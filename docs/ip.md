## Patent

### Edge-Based Clinical Intelligence via Graph Neural Networks

- **Application Number:** 202541127477  
- **Jurisdiction:** India  
- **Status:** Published  

---

## Scope of Patent

The patent relates to a **plug-and-play, on-device clinical intelligence system** capable of:

- Performing real-time clinical data processing entirely on-device  
- Converting multilingual interactions into structured clinical data  
- Maintaining a continuously evolving clinical state representation  
- Evaluating patient safety through a tiered decision framework  
- Operating without transmitting sensitive patient data to external systems  

The system integrates conversational processing, physiological sensing, clinical reasoning, and audit mechanisms within a single edge-based device.
